About the Brand

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in London, LUXE GLOBAL started as a small curated boutique connecting fashion lovers with independent ateliers across Europe.

Our founder spent years in luxury retail in Milan and Paris and saw a gap ,extraordinary craftsmanship was trapped behind borders. Today we partner with over 200 certified brands across 35 countries.

We believe geography should never limit taste. Whether you're in Maseru or Manhattan, you deserve access to the world's finest fashion.
